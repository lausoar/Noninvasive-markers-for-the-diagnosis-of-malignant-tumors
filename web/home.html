 Introduction

The incidence rate of cancer has been increasing all over the world , at the same time, the mortality rate has also increased rapidly. It is a serious threat to human hea-lth and life, and is one of the main causes of human death.

MicroRNA is crucial for understanding biological pr-ocesses and pathogenesis of cancers. Which can be used as molecular biomarkers of malignant tumors.

It is very important to develop a database containi-ng both cancer tissue and blood microRNA information. This database collects and collates human cancer tissue blood microRNAs related to 33 cancer types, such as bre-ast invasive carcinoma, cholangioca-rcinoma, glioma, etc. The data were obtained by analysis and reading a large number of literatures in pubmed, including expression he-at map, differential microRNAs, ROC curve analysis, SNP location, survival analysis, target gene, decision tree, entr-opy weight, functional analysis.
